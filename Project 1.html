Seaforth Station Upgrade

GaysNet will propose a 276 million dollar plan that will involve the upgrading of Seaforth station:

  • 14 bus stands, including being served by the CircleRoute. All routes terminating at Kelmscott will now instead terminate here. 12 of these stands are set-down only, the 13th is rail replacement, and the 14th is every single other route.
  • 1500 parking spaces in a multi-storey car park. Out of those, only one is an ACROD space, and it is the third-furthest spot from the actual station.
  • Passenger amenities including toilets, kiosk and ticketing.
  • Not wheelchair accessible because there will only be one set of stairs as an entrance to the station which can only access Platform 2 and not Platform 1. Trains will only stop at Platform 1 and not at Platform 2.
  • Ignore what I said because Platform 2 is being renumbered to Platform 9 and 3/4.
  • 5-zone fare to Perth because fuck the fare cap teehee
  • Level crossings will not be removed.
  • New S pattern to replace C Pattern in peak: express from McIver to Seaforth. Only inbound S pattern trains will stop at Perth Stadium on stadium days.
  • A massive fucking Walmart bag will be a statue blocking the Platform 1 entrance (this is why you cannot access it).
  • For every dollar you donate to the project, we will cut down a tree.
  • One bicycle space where locks are not allowed.
  • No transit guards. They will be guarding Challis instead.
  • New high frequency route 944 from Seaforth to Success Hill express. Possible deviation K via Caves Road.
  • Addition of a helipad.
  • All signs, posters and warnings will be replaced with emojis.
